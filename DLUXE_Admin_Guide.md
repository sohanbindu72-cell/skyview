# 🛫 D'LUXE VIP Services - Administrator Guide

Welcome to the D'LUXE VIP Services Administrative Dashboard. This centralized hub provides you with comprehensive control over your global operations, packages, locations, and real-time flight bookings.

---

## 1. Navigating the Dashboard

Your dashboard is divided into five core operational pillars, accessible via the main sidebar:
* **Reservations:** Live tracking of all customer flight bookings.
* **Leads:** Manage incoming inquiries and unconverted contacts.
* **Packages:** Configure your core service tiers (e.g., VIP, Premium, Standard).
* **Locations:** Design your geographic network, controlling exactly which airports are supported.
* **Customers:** Directory of registered platform users.

---

## 2. Managing Reservations

The Reservations page is your core operational hub. 
* **Global Filter:** Quickly locate a booking by typing the customer's name, email, or flight number into the search bar.
* **Editing Status:** Click the **Edit (Pencil)** icon to update a booking's operational status (`Pending`, `Confirmed`, `Completed`, `Cancelled`) and its payment status (`Paid`, `Unpaid`, `Refunded`).
* **View Details:** Click the **Eye** icon to open the full Read-Only view of the passenger's itinerary, routing, and selected service packages. 

---

## 3. Configuring Locations & Airports

The Locations system is highly flexible and relies on a "Country -> Airports" structure.

### Adding a Location
1. Click **Add Location**.
2. Enter the overarching Country Name and select a Flag Icon.
3. Add as many exact Airports as necessary (e.g., Punta Cana (PUJ)).

### Airport Matrices (Crucial)
Inside the Airport editor, you will see a list of all active Packages.
* **Toggling Availability:** Click any package to toggle it Orange (Available) or Gray (Restricted). If a package is restricted, customers cannot book it at this specific airport.
* **Custom Overrides:** If an available package is more expensive at this specific airport than the global Base Price, type the override amount into the `Price` box. The system will automatically charge this custom rate for this airport.

---

## 4. Configuring Packages

Packages are the service tiers you offer globally.
* **Creating a Package:** Define a global Base Price, load it with features, and configure its rank order.
* **Analyzing Package Data:** Click the **Eye** icon next to any package to open its dedicated Data View. 
  * You will immediately see its **Historical Revenue** and total generated bookings.
  * *Tip:* Clicking the "Historical Bookings" box will instantly teleport you to the Reservations page, pre-filtered to show only bookings utilizing this specific package!
  * **Coverage Matrix:** The table at the bottom of the Package View consolidates its geographic availability, displaying its exact pricing at every supported airport globally.

---

## 5. Leads & CRM

When a customer submits a direct inquiry from the landing page without completing checkout, it lands in the **Leads** dashboard.
* Track the date the inquiry was made.
* Utilize the dropdown to manually advance the lead's status (`Incoming`, `Contacted`, `Booked`).
* Click the **Eye** icon to view their requested routing and passenger headcount to formulate a targeted sales follow-up.
